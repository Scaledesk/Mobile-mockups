How to Mirror Your iPhone using the Solstice Pod

Step 1: open settings on your iphone

step 2: goto wifi and turn it on

Step 3: select solstice pod network

Step 4: once connected to the wifi network press home button

Step 5: open airplay settings from bottom drawer on your iphone

Step 6: select mirror as your device and turn on screen sharing. your screen is mirrored
